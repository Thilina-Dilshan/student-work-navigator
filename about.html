About This Project

Student Work Navigator is a university project built to make the German student-job market less confusing for international students in Magdeburg.

Why we built this

Every year, hundreds of international students arrive in Magdeburg to study at Otto von Guericke University and Hochschule Magdeburg-Stendal. Many want to work part-time — for income, for experience, or simply to build a life here — but the German job market comes with unfamiliar rules, vocabulary, and expectations.

This site was created as a university project to bring together, in one place, the practical information that's usually scattered across university pages, government sites, and word of mouth: what job types exist, how to apply the German way, and where to actually look in Magdeburg.

Our approach

What guided this project

Practical over theoretical

Every page points to something you can actually do — a real portal, a concrete step, a specific document.

Accurate & up to date

Legal details (like work-day limits) are explained clearly, with a reminder to always confirm current rules with your International Office.

Built with students, for students

Feedback from real international students — collected through the Survey Results page — directly shapes what we add next.
