Who this guide is for

Are you an "international student" here?

For this guide, an international student is anyone who:

  • Is already enrolled at a university in Magdeburg (e.g. Otto von Guericke University, Hochschule Magdeburg-Stendal) — this site does not cover student visa applications.
  • Holds a non-German (or non-EU) passport, or a German residence permit issued for study purposes.
  • Wants to work legally alongside their studies — whether that's a HiWi position, a Werkstudent role, a Minijob, or an internship.

We explain German-specific rules (like the 120-day / 240-half-day limit for non-EU students) in plain language, so you can focus on studying and building your career without guesswork.
