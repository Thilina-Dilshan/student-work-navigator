Where to Find Student Jobs

Real, Magdeburg-relevant platforms and contacts — no guesswork, just where to actually look.

University Portals

Your first stop should always be official university channels — postings here are aimed specifically at students and are the most trustworthy.

  • OVGU JobTeaser — the official job portal of Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg: internships, Werkstudent roles and graduate jobs, mostly from regional employers. Log in with your university account.
    Visit OVGU JobTeaser
  • OVGU Career Service — workshops, CV checks and an International Career Service specifically supporting international students entering the German job market.
    Visit International Career Service
  • H2 Career Center (Hochschule Magdeburg-Stendal) — runs the “Nachwuchsmarkt” job portal plus dedicated advising for international and refugee-background students.
    Visit H2 Career Center

Job Platforms

General job boards with strong listings for student jobs, Werkstudent roles and internships in Germany.

  • LinkedIn Jobs — filter by “Magdeburg” + “Werkstudent”/“Student”; also great for networking with alumni.
    Search LinkedIn Jobs
  • Indeed Deutschland — large volume of Minijob, Werkstudent and internship postings, searchable by city.
    Search Indeed
  • StepStone — one of Germany's largest job portals, strong for Werkstudent and internship roles.
    Search StepStone
  • Bundesagentur für Arbeit (Jobbörse) — Germany's official employment agency job board, including Minijobs.
    Search Jobbörse

Local Employers & Agencies in Magdeburg

Staffing agencies and local employer directories that regularly place students in Magdeburg.

  • Randstad Magdeburg — one of Germany's largest staffing agencies, with a local branch placing candidates in part-time, temporary and permanent roles around Magdeburg.
    Randstad Magdeburg jobs
  • Adecco Germany — another major staffing agency; search by city to find Minijob and temporary placements in Magdeburg and Sachsen-Anhalt.
    Adecco job search
  • IHK Magdeburg (Chamber of Commerce) — represents around 50,000 regional businesses; a useful directory for direct or cold applications to local employers.
    IHK Magdeburg

University Events & Communities

Networking is often the fastest route to a good student job — many roles are never publicly advertised.

  • OVGU Career Service events — the Campus Pitch and Firmenkontaktmesse career fairs bring regional employers on campus to meet students, including sessions aimed at internationals.
    Career Service events
  • Erasmus Student Network (ESN) — find your nearest local ESN section for social events, trips and a strong peer network of international students in the region.
    Find your ESN section
  • StuRa OVGU (Student Council) — the highest student representative body at OVGU; a good source of on-campus community groups and support.
    StuRa OVGU
