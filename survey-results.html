Survey Results & Feedback

Read what other students said, and add your own experience — your answers are saved to a shared Google Sheet to help future students.

Share your experience

Answered honestly, your feedback helps us improve this guide and helps other international students know what to expect. It only takes a minute.

How this works

Your response is sent directly to a Google Sheet maintained by the project team using a Google Apps Script connector — no account or sign-up needed.

  • We don't collect emails or contact details unless you choose to include them in your message.
  • Responses may be featured (anonymised or with your first name) on the Experiences page.
  • This is a non-commercial university project — your data is never sold or used for advertising.

Questions about your data? Email the project team.
